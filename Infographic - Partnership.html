LAYER 4 OF 7

⛔ Deep Dives: What Partnership Is Not

These behavioral misunderstandings keep believers trapped in the cycle of waiting.

NOT PASSIVE RECEPTION

+

The believer who expects God to produce the result while they function as a spectator has misunderstood the arrangement. "Faith by itself, if it does not have works, is dead" — meaning functionally inoperative.

NOT THE "IRRESPONSIBLE AMEN"

+

Enthusiastically receiving a prophetic word in a church service while returning to the same patterns of inaction, negligence, and undeveloped capacity is not faith. It is receiving an admission letter and never showing up to class.

NOT A SUBSTITUTE FOR PREPARATION

+

Prayer is essential. But prayer that replaces preparation creates an imbalance. Pray for the business, AND build the business plan. Pray for restoration, AND take the practical step to reconcile. Prayer opens the door; partnership walks through it.
