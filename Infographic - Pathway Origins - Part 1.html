🧩 Experiential Deep-Dives & Labs

TAP: THE SCRIPTURAL TRUTH TABLE

+

The iron cage was constructed using out-of-context religious fear. Below is the direct scriptural evidence that dismantles each limiting belief.

THE LIMITING BELIEF (THE LIE) THE COVENANT REALITY (THE SCRIPTURE)
Success is Pride. To want influence or resource is a worldly ambition that threatens your salvation. "You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden... let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven."Matthew 5:14-16
Smallness is Humility. Poverty and obscurity are the highest forms of spiritual purity and devotion. "But remember the Lord your God, for it is He who gives you the ability to produce wealth, and so confirms His covenant, which He swore to your ancestors."Deuteronomy 8:18
Wealth is Evil. Resources inherently corrupt the soul, so believers should avoid financial accumulation. "For the love of money is a root of all kinds of evil. Some people, eager for money, have wandered from the faith." (The *love* of money is the root, not the tool itself).1 Timothy 6:10
Suffering is Disqualification. If your life is brutal, God has rejected you or is punishing your hidden sin. "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose." The process builds character; it is not rejection.Romans 8:28

TAP: WHY LEGALISM DRIVES MEN AWAY

+

When relationship is measured entirely by what you *do not do*, the believer is forced to focus constantly on sin rather than on the Saviour. It creates an impossible standard. Inside that cage, I found no evidence that God was real. All I found were rules. All I found was guilt.

⚠️ Warning: The Illusion of Freedom

I thought that if I could just get rich, I could finally be free from the surveillance camera in the sky. But cutting off God doesn't give you freedom. It just transfers you to a different warden. The prison of futility is worse than the cage of rules.

Rebellion is not a strategy. It is an act of desperation.
