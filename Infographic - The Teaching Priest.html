LAYER 4 OF 7

📚 The Cost of Lopsided Growth

A critical crisis in the modern charismatic church is lopsided spiritual growth. The teaching priest is mandated to provide the whole counsel of God.

DANGER: THEOLOGICAL IMBALANCE

+

A church versed in prosperity but thin on character. A church equipped to rebuke demons but unprepared to build institutions. A building with strong walls but no foundation will collapse under the same pressure a fully-built structure easily manages.

DANGER: REVELATION WITHOUT PURPOSE

+

Revelation without purpose becomes a psychological burden. Prosperity without purpose leads to hedonism. Power without purpose leads to spiritual abuse. The "what" must constantly tie to the "why."

DANGER: KNOWLEDGE WITHOUT MASTERY

+

There is a life-determining difference between knowing a scriptural principle and executing it to predictability. The rebuke in Hebrews 5 is for stagnant development. The standard is mastery, not memory.

LAYER 5 OF 7

⚠️ The Weight of the Office

The teaching priest wants to produce believers of extraordinary spiritual stature. But the impartation is always limited by the level of the carrier. Before the public platform is the private altar.

The priest sharing from the accumulation of old insights from years ago is giving the congregation recycled oil. Recycled oil lights a lamp temporarily, but it does not sustain the fire.

LAYER 6 OF 7

🪞 Diagnostic For Believers

Under whose formation are you currently sitting? Not whose services you occasionally attend or podcast you play—under whose systematic formation are you being built?

Can you identify specifically what you know now that you did not know one year ago, and what you can do now that you could not do one year ago?
