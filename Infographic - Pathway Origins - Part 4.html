Discernment vs. The Overactive Imagination

"If a bird flew a certain way, I’d ask what it 'meant' spiritually. If I stubbed my toe, I’d look for a spiritual law I had broken. I was trying to spiritualize every single detail of reality. And that is the fastest path to deception."

LAYER 1 OF 7

🔑 One-Line Thesis

Hearing the voice of God requires a systematic filter of peace and joy; otherwise, you become a victim of your own hyper-spiritualized imagination.

LAYER 2 OF 7

"I had to find a system. I needed a way to discern the voice of the Lord that wasn't based on my own overactive imagination. My method became very simple: I would find a silent, peaceful place. I’d pick up a pen and a piece of paper. And I’d write."
