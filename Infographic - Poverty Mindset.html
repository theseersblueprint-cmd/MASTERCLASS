LAYER 1 OF 7

🐫 Wealth's Baggage

"The camel can enter the gate. But not with the baggage. And not standing tall."

The "Eye of the Needle" was a narrow pedestrian gate in ancient Jerusalem built for late merchants. A camel could only fit if every piece of its baggage was removed and it crawled through on its knees.

This is not a verse about impossibility. This is a verse about protocol. Wealth cannot enter if it carries pride, misplaced identity, or corruption.

THE FALSE HUMILITY

When a wrong belief makes you feel superior and spiritual, it becomes almost impossible to touch. Poverty is not humility, and humility is not poverty. The earth gets shaped by whoever shows up to occupy it.
