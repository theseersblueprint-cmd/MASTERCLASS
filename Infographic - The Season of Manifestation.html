🧩 The Most Dangerous Season

THE THREAT OF THE EGO

The season of manifestation amplifies everything. What was hidden is now visible. The grace for visibility is highly dangerous if the self is still alive.

The version of you still trying to prove doubters wrong finds this season the most tempting environment ever. It corrupts the motive and aborts a season that took years to build.

THE OTHERS

It Is Not For You

+

The visibility, resources, and influence do not exist for your comfort. They exist because there are people on the other side of your assignment who desperately need what only your specific journey sets you up to provide. The person who earned compassion through failure is the one who can trusted with visibility.
