The Scars of Victory (The Nail Marks)

+

When Jesus rose from the dead, the nail marks in His hands remained. They were physically present on His glorified body. Yet nobody looks at those scars and concludes that Jesus lost.

Your history is real. Your scars are real. But they are not evidence that you belong to the enemy. They are evidence that you survived what tried to destroy you. Change your belief about your scars, and you change their legal authority over your life.

LAYER 5 OF 7

⚠️ The Critical Warning

⚠  PACIFICATION PRODUCES CAPTIVITY

There is no negotiation table in the Kingdom of God. You cannot sit across from darkness and present offers. If you attempt to pacify a spirit through cultural norms, temporary compromises, or cowardly prayer, you only manage your captivity.

Unbelief Grants Access. The enemy cannot override the finished work of Christ. But if he can convince you that his shadows are permanent, your unbelief grants him the legal access he otherwise lacks.
